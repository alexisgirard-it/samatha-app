What it is

A sitting timer with a singing bowl and optional vigilance bells; the ten-stage map as an interactive painting; a practice journal with CSV export and private iCloud backup; and an honest check-in: you note the signs you're actually experiencing and the app can suggest a stage — but it never advances you on its own. You confirm mastery yourself, against the criteria in the book. It was built specifically so it would never turn practice into a number to chase.

It also carries a short safety note for destabilizing experiences — a serious tool should carry the book's guardrails, not just its techniques.
